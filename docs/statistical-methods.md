# Statistical Methods

This document summarizes the statistical methods used for the
oncology ORR efficacy table.

---

## ORR Estimation

- ORR is calculated as the proportion of subjects with CR or PR
- Estimates are produced by treatment group

---

## Confidence Interval for ORR

- A two-sided 95% confidence interval is calculated for ORR
- The Clopper–Pearson exact binomial method is used

---

## Difference in ORR

- The difference in ORR between active treatment and placebo
  is calculated
- An approximate 95% confidence interval is derived using a
  normal approximation

---

## P-value

- A Chi-square / Cochran–Armitage trend test is used to assess
  treatment differences
- The resulting p-value is reported in the table
